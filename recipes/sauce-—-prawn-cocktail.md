# Sauce - Prawn Cocktail

## ingredients

- 200 ml double cream
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
- 1 tsp tabasco sauce
- 1/4 tsp salt

## steps

1. Add all ingredients to a small bowl and stir until combined and smooth.
