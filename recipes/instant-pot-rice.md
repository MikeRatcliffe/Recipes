# Instant Pot Rice

## ingredients

- 1 cup (230g) Jasmine rice
- 1 cup (250ml) cold water
- 1/2 tsp salt

## steps

1. Add 1 cup (230g) rice and 1 cup (250ml) cold water to your Instant Pot.
2. Close the lid, turn Venting Knob to Sealing Position and Pressure Cook at High Pressure for 3 minutes, then NaturalÂ Release for 10 minutes.
3. Done.
