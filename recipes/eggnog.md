# Eggnog

![Eggnog](images/eggnog.jpg)

## ingredients

- 250 ml milk
- 250 ml cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 teaspoon stevia
- 1 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1 teaspoon rum extract
- Pinch of salt

## steps

1. Blend and enjoy!
