## description

This ranch seasoning mix keeps well and can be used anytime to make ranch dressing, dip, or a savory seasoning blend.

## ingredients

- 1 tbsp dried parsley
- 1 tsp dried chives
- 2 tsp garlic powder
- 2 tsp onion powder
- 3 tsp dried dill
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp MSG
- 1/4 tsp citric acid
- 25 g powdered buttermilk

## steps

### Main

1. Mix everything together and store in an airtight jar.

### To Make Ranch Dressing

1. Combine:
   - 1 tbsp seasoning mix
   - 120 g mayonnaise
   - 120 g sour cream
   - 80–120 ml milk (less milk for a thicker dressing)
