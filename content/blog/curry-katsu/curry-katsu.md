## description

An oh so tasty, yet super simple katsu sauce recipe.

## ingredients

- 6 tbsp cornflour
- 175 ml water
- 2 tbsp oil
- 2 onion (finely chopped)
- 4 medium carrots (sliced)
- 5 tbsp curry powder
- 2 tsp garlic powder
- salt to taste
- 1 tsp garam masala (optional)
- 1,600 ml chicken or veg stock
- 3 tbsp honey

## steps

1. Add the oil and onion to a pan and fry until translucent.
2. Add the carrot and fry for a couple more minutes.
3. Stir in the curry powder, garlic powder, salt and garam masala (if using).
4. Pour on the stock and simmer for 7-10 minutes until the carrots are tender.
5. Blend using a stick blender.
6. Mix the cornflour and water and add to the pan to thicken.
7. Add add the honey.
8. Pour on a little more stock if the sauce seems too thick.
