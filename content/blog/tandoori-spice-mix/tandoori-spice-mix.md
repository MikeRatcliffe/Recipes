## ingredients

### Tandoori Marinade

- 1 tsp ground ginger
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp ground cloves

## steps

1. Mix all ingredients together.

## notes

Makes about 2 tablespoons.
