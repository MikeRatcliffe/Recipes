## ingredients

- 2 tbsp plain greek yogurt
- 40g rolled oats
- 30g mixed berries
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- 1 tsp cinnamon

## steps

1. Mix together the oats, yogurt, mixed berries, almond butter and cinnamon.
2. Place in a mason jar.
3. Store overnight in the fridge and enjoy in the morning.
