## description

This Caribbean Brown Oxtail Stew is a rich, savory dish of tender oxtail simmered with vegetables and aromatic herbs, mild and non-spicy, perfect over rice or mashed potatoes.

## ingredients

- 1.2 kg oxtail
- 30 g butter
- 1 large onion (chopped)
- 3 cloves garlic (minced)
- 2 medium carrots (chopped)
- 1 stalk celery (chopped)
- 1 bell pepper (chopped)
- 400g tomatoes
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 500 ml beef stock
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tsp allspice
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- 2 tbsp tomato puree
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar or white vinegar
- Fresh parsley or thyme for garnish

## steps

### Prep & Brown Oxtail

1. Pat the oxtail dry and season generously with salt and black pepper.
2. Turn your Ninja Foodi to Sauté / Browning mode and heat the oil.
3. Brown oxtail pieces on all sides (about 4–5 min per batch). Remove and set aside.

### Sauté Vegetables

1. In the same pot, add onion, garlic, carrots, celery, and bell pepper.
2. Sauté for 4–5 minutes until slightly softened.
3. Stir in tomato puree and cook for 1 minute to deepen flavor.

### Deglaze & Build Flavor

1. Add soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, and vinegar.
2. Stir to deglaze the pot, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom.
3. Add chopped tomatoes, thyme, bay leaves, allspice, and beef stock.

### Pressure Cook

1. Return oxtail to the pot, submerging it in the liquid.
2. Seal the Ninja Foodi lid and set to Pressure Cook for 1 hour.
3. Once done, allow natural release for 10–15 minutes.
4. Check flavours and season more as needed.
