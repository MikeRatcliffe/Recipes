## description

Here’s a Southern-style pig feet stew. This version is rich, smoky, and soul-food authentic.

## ingredients

- 1 kg pig feet (cleaned and split)
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 medium onion (chopped)
- 4 cloves garlic (minced)
- 1 green bell pepper (chopped)
- 2 celery stalks (chopped)
- 1 smoked turkey leg or 1 smoked sausage (sliced)
- 4 cups chicken broth
- 400 g diced tomatoes
- 2 medium potatoes (cubed)
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1–2 tsp Cajun seasoning
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- Dash of hot sauce (optional)

## steps

### Clean And Blanch

1. Rinse pig feet well.
2. Place in pressure cooker, cover with water.
3. Pressure cook on HIGH for 5 minutes.
4. Quick release and drain.
5. Rinse pot and pig feet.

### Sauté aromatics

1. Select Sauté function (Medium).
2. Add oil, onion, bell pepper, celery.
3. Cook for 3–4 minutes until softened.
4. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.

### Pressure cook

1. Add pig feet, smoked meat, tomatoes, broth, and all seasonings except potatoes and vinegar.
2. Stir well.
3. Close lid, set valve to seal.
4. Select Pressure → HIGH → 60 minutes.

### Release and add potatoes

1. Natural release for 10–15 minutes, then quick release remaining pressure.
2. Add potatoes.
3. Pressure cook on HIGH for 5 minutes.
4. Quick release.

### Finish and thicken

1. Stir in apple cider vinegar and hot sauce.
2. Turn on Sauté mode and simmer 5–10 minutes if you want thicker gravy.
