## ingredients

- 40g chia seeds
- 240 ml coconut milk
- 8 raspberries

## steps

1. Mix together the chia seeds and coconut milk. Place the mixture in a tightly closed mason jar in the fridge overnight.
2. Top with some raspberries and enjoy!

## notes

- 250 kcal
- 10g carbohydrates/serving
- Serves 1
